About

I'm Charles, an aspiring developer from Lewis County, Missouri. Building things has always been my passion— now I'm building clean, mobile-first landing pages designed to make your business look professional and drive conversions. If you want a page that represents your brand at its best, let's talk.

What I Deliver

  • Mobile-first layout: looks great on phones and desktop
  • Fast + readable: clean spacing, clear typography
  • Accessible: easy to use for everyone (keyboard-friendly)
  • Conversion-focused: clear sections and strong calls-to-action
  • Built with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

  2. Design + draft: I mock up the layout and copy, then we refine it fast.
  3. Build + launch: I ship the page and help you get it live (hosting optional).
